More about

The Thomas Nelson Study Bible (BETN) is a landmark in the history of annotated Bibles. Unlike other study Bibles, BETN has biblical theology as its guiding principle. With this unique proposal, it allows the reader to ponder the individual stories and themes of Scripture, while also observing how they all fit into God's grand narrative throughout his Word. Which promotes a clearer understanding of the Bible - not just its parts, but also its entire story. Follow the plan of redemption as it is revealed in the Scriptures.
